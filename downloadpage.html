System Requirements

Processor:

  • Minimum: Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 processor
  • Recommended: Intel Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 processor
  • ModelMaster may also be compatible with equivalent processors from other manufacturers.

RAM (Random Access Memory):

  • Minimum: 8 GB RAM
  • Recommended: 16 GB RAM
  • ModelMaster requires sufficient RAM for smooth rendering and manipulation of 3D models.

Storage:

  • Minimum: 271 MB of available storage space
  • ModelMaster is designed to be lightweight and efficient, requiring only a small amount of storage for installation.
  • Additional storage may be necessary for saving project files and assets, depending on the complexity of the models created.

These system requirements ensure that users can run ModelMaster smoothly on their devices and have enough resources to create and manipulate 3D models effectively.
